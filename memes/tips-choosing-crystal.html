Choosing Your Crystal
  • ✦Notice what catches your eye — your intuition already knows. The crystal that draws you in is usually the one you need.
  • ✦Hold it in your hand — close your eyes. Does it feel warm? Tingly? Calming? Your body will tell you.
  • ✦Think about what you need right now — healing? Protection? Love? Let your current chapter guide your choice.
  • ✦Don't overthink it — there's no wrong crystal. If you're drawn to it, it has something to offer you.
  • ✦Let it choose you — sometimes a crystal falls off the shelf, keeps appearing, or a friend gifts it. Pay attention to those signs.
