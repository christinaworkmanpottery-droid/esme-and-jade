Manifesting with Crystals
  • 1.Get clear on your intention — write it down. Vague wishes get vague results.
  • 2.Choose a crystal that matches — citrine for abundance, rose quartz for love, amethyst for clarity.
  • 3.Hold it and speak your intention — say it out loud. Your voice carries energy.
  • 4.Keep it close daily — wear it, carry it, place it where you'll see it. Consistency matters.
  • 5.Trust and release — do the work, then let go. Crystals amplify, but you are the magic.
