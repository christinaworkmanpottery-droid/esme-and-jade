Pottery Classes

Learn to Create with Clay
$95 per personAll tools, supplies & firing included

One-on-one or small group 2-hour pottery classes in my studio in Culver City — or I come to you! As long as there's a table, we can do pottery. Each person needs about 2 feet of table space (think placemat-sized).

Class is 1.5 hours of hands-on creating + 30 minutes for setup & cleanup. We'll cover a quick start-to-finish runthrough of turning clay into a functional piece.

What You Get:
  • Learn basic hand building techniques
  • Make 1–3 pieces to take home
  • Pieces are finished & ready in 2–3 weeks after class
  • All tools, supplies & kiln firing included
  • Groups of 2+ get 10% off

Tip: Before class, think about what you'd like to make — bowls, plates, cups, etc. Search "handmade pottery" online for endless inspiration!

Perfect For:

Birthday parties · Date night · Girl's night · Build & sip · Team building · Kids ages 4+ (under 12 with parent supervision)

Off-Site Parties & Take-Home Kits

We offer off-site pottery parties for all ages — adult packages include light snacks & wine. We also have pottery take-home kits — pick up clay, create at home, bring pieces back for firing & glazing. Email for details!

We use medium-fire clay & glazes fired to cone 5.
