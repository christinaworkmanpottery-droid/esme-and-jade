Pottery Classes

Learn to Create with Clay
$95 per personAll tools, supplies & firing included

One-on-one or small group 2-hour pottery classes in my studio in Culver City — or I come to you! All you need is a table (about 2 ft per person). Class is 1.5 hours hands-on + 30 min setup & cleanup. Learn the basics of turning clay into a functional piece from start to finish.

What You Get:
  • Learn basic hand building techniques
  • Make 1–3 pieces — finished & ready in 2–3 weeks
  • All tools, supplies & kiln firing included
  • Groups of 2+ get 10% off
Perfect For:

Birthday parties · Date night · Girl's night · Build & sip · Team building · Kids 4+ (under 12 w/ parent)

Off-site parties available for all ages (snacks & wine for adults). Take-home kits also available — create at home, bring back for firing! Email for details.
